Services

I help research organizations, policy groups, and nonprofits turn complex data into clear, defensible insights — from evaluation design to final deliverables. My niche is spatial and regional economic-impact analysis and health-economics burden modeling for agencies, foundations, and health systems.

How it works

  1. Intro call

    A free 30-minute call to understand your question, your data, the timeline, and any constraints.

  2. Scoped proposal

    A short written proposal with the approach, deliverables, timeline, and a fixed price — no surprises.

  3. Analysis

    Transparent, reproducible work with checkpoints along the way so you stay in the loop.

  4. Deliverable & walkthrough

    Final memo, model, or visuals — plus a live walkthrough and the documented code behind it.
